# Venue

SPIN 2026 is co-located with [ETAPS 2026](https://etaps.org/2026/) in Torino, Italy.
For information on travel and accommodation we refer to the information provided on the ETAPS website.

# Registration

Registration will be handled as part of the ETAPS registration process. Participants who register for the main ETAPS days will be allowed to attend SPIN. Participants who would only like to attend SPIN will have the option to register SPIN only at a reduced rate. This SPIN-only registration does not include the ETAPS Banquet and Welcome Reception (tickets for these can be purchased separately), but does include access to all other ETAPS sessions during the days on which SPIN takes place.
